About Us

About Kasi Tech Hub

KASI TECH HUB (PTY) LTD is a South African digital skills training and technology solutions company based in Refilwe, Cullinan, Gauteng. We are a community-based initiative created to help bridge the digital divide.

Founded by Thabang Seloane, we provide practical and affordable technology training to young people, job seekers, students, entrepreneurs, and members of the community. Our goal is to equip participants with the knowledge and confidence required to use technology effectively in education, employment, entrepreneurship, and everyday life.

We believe that access to digital knowledge creates opportunities for employment, entrepreneurship, education, and economic growth.

Excellence Innovation Integrity Community Empowerment Professionalism Continuous Learning

🎯 Mission

To provide accessible, practical, and community-focused digital skills training that improves employment opportunities and promotes digital inclusion.

🔭 Vision

To become a trusted community technology centre that empowers people through digital education, innovation, and practical technology support — South Africa’s leading township-based digital skills academy.
